GAA Scor Revision Aid
=====================

## Overview

A revision aid for the Trath na gCeist (Quiz) section of the GAA Scor competition, focusing on Gaelic and Ladies Football questions.

## Contents

- `gaelic_and_ladies_football_questions.md` - Quiz questions organised in rounds of 10, covering men's and ladies Gaelic football. Each round includes 2 questions as Gaeilge.

## Format

- 10 questions per round
- Questions 9 and 10 in each round are as Gaeilge (in Irish)
- Mix of men's and ladies football topics
- Topics covered: championship winners, venues, managers, sponsors, players, records, history, club championships, college competitions

## Sources

Questions are based on verified facts from GAA.ie, LGFA, Wikipedia, RTE Sport, and other reliable sources. All facts have been cross-referenced where possible.
