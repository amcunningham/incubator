About

This app helps GAA clubs and individuals prepare for Scor Trath na gCeist (Senior Quiz) competitions at county, provincial and All-Ireland level.

You can practise solo with flashcards or run full timed quiz rounds in the format used at real Scor competitions — 10 questions per round, with questions 9 and 10 as Gaeilge.

The question bank covers all the standard Scor categories and is updated regularly. If you have questions to contribute, use the Submit a Question option on the homescreen.

You can give feedback on individual questions and suggest edits by clicking on the Feedback button beside each question. Some of the questions might be too easy. Let us know!

You can give us general feedback by clicking on Send Feedback from the homepage. All thoughts and ideas welcome.

This site has been started by Anne Marie Cunningham, of Atticall GAC on 21st February 2026.
